Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Social Publisher collects, uses, and stores information when you use the service to connect social media accounts and publish content using official APIs.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic account information returned by connected platform APIs.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required for authorized publishing.
  • Uploaded video files, thumbnails, titles, descriptions, hashtags, and scheduling data.
  • Operational logs related to publishing status, failures, and API responses.

2. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate connected accounts through OAuth.
  • To upload and publish content to selected platforms.
  • To show job status, delivery results, and error messages.
  • To improve reliability, security, and abuse prevention.

3. OAuth and Credentials

The service is designed to use OAuth. Platform passwords are not intended to be stored in the application. OAuth tokens may be stored securely to maintain authorized access.

4. Data Sharing

Data is shared only as necessary to interact with official platform APIs such as YouTube and TikTok. The service does not sell personal information.

5. Data Retention

Uploaded files, metadata, logs, and OAuth tokens may be retained as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce policies.

6. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored data, including secure transport and encrypted storage of sensitive authorization material where supported by the deployment.

7. Your Choices

You may disconnect linked accounts, request deletion of stored data where supported, and stop using the service at any time.

8. Third-Party Platforms

Use of connected platforms remains subject to their own privacy policies, developer policies, and content moderation rules.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the service operator through the official support channel associated with this application.
